# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
- **B2C Consumers**: Health-conscious household decision-makers in India seeking pure, nutrient-rich, chemical-free cooking oil (Jeevan Rekha Rice Bran Oil / Mustard Oil) to protect family heart health.
- **B2B Industrial Partners**: Commercial buyers, animal feed manufacturers (DORB for cattle/poultry/fish feed), and cosmetic/pharmaceutical/polish manufacturers looking for high-quality, high-melting Rice Bran Wax, Gums, and Lecithin.
- **Distributors & Retailers**: FMCG partners looking to onboard and distribute a trusted, high-volume consumer wellness brand.

## Product Purpose
To showcase AB Udyog Pvt. Ltd. as a state-of-the-art, premium FMCG manufacturer and establish Jeevan Rekha as Eastern India's golden standard of edible oil purity. The website serves to tell the corporate story of scaling from a 300 TPD B2B extraction unit to a premium B2C household wellness brand, providing complete product specifications, technical quality details, and partnership pipelines.

## Brand Personality
- **Premium Editorial**: Sophisticated and elegant, utilizing serif headings, ample whitespace, and clean data tables to feel premium and trustworthy rather than cheap and commercial.
- **Scientific Integrity**: Focused on actual specs, extraction science (physical vs chemical refining), and on-site lab parameters (10,000+ PPM Oryzanol, gas chromatography checks).
- **Industrial Scale & Trust**: Bold metrics showing massive, automated capacity (300 TPD solvent plant, 150 TPD refinery in Kolkata) that assure B2B partners of reliable supply.

## Anti-references
- **SaaS Clichés**: Mono-tone beige/sand colors, generic abstract shapes, rounded blob buttons, and "AI slop" like tracked tiny kickers/eyebrows above every section.
- **Monotone Layouts**: Bland gray-on-gray structures, generic icons without text labels, or flat card grids that lack brand identity.
- **Vague Copy**: Using words like "streamline", "empower", or "supercharge" instead of specific details like "Continuous Physical Refining" and "10,000+ PPM Oryzanol".

## Design Principles
1. **Scientific Transparency**: Lead with technical specifications, certifications, and manufacturing facts. Technical sheets should be interactive, readable, and feel authoritative.
2. **The Golden Path**: Visually differentiate between the consumer flagship brand (Jeevan Rekha in forest green and warm amber/gold) and industrial derivatives (structured dark slate and technical data layouts).
3. **Purity of Movement**: Use smooth, intentional scroll reveals and page transitions (via Framer Motion) to mimic the fluidity of refined oil, avoiding cheap, jittery bounce animations.
4. **Contrast & Authority**: High text-to-bg contrast (≥4.5:1) using rich off-black text on off-white warm slate backgrounds to ensure legibility and a premium editorial feel.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- **Contrast**: Strictly conform to WCAG AA guidelines with text color contrast ≥4.5:1 (large text ≥3:1).
- **Screen Reader Support**: All icons must have associated text labels or aria-labels; dynamic tab views must have proper aria roles.
- **Reduced Motion**: Respect `@media (prefers-reduced-motion: reduce)` by disabling or simplifying Framer Motion transitions.
